# Datasets

This pipeline was tested on the following datasets. Download each and place images in `data/input/<dataset_name>/`. 

A sample of 45 images from U45 is already included in `data/input/U45/` for quick testing.

---

## Labeled Datasets

**U45** : Li, H., Li, J., & Wang, W. (2019). *A Fusion Adversarial Underwater Image Enhancement Network with a Public Test Dataset*. arXiv:1906.06819. https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.1906.06819

**UID2021** : Hou, G., Li, Y., Yang, H., Li, K., & Pan, Z. (2022). *UID2021: An Underwater Image Dataset for Evaluation of No-reference Quality Assessment Metrics*. arXiv:2204.08813. https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2204.08813

**UCCS (from RUIE)** : Liu, R., Fan, X., Zhu, M., Hou, M., & Luo, Z. (2020). *Real-World Underwater Enhancement: Challenges, Benchmarks, and Solutions Under Natural Light*. IEEE TCSVT, 30(12), 4861–4875. https://doi.org/10.1109/TCSVT.2019.2963772

## Synthesized Dataset

**UWCNN** : Li, C., Anwar, S., & Porikli, F. (2020). *Underwater scene prior inspired deep underwater image and video enhancement*. Pattern Recognition, 98, 107038. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.patcog.2019.107038

## Turbidity-Referenced Dataset

**Turbidivision** : Rudy, I. M., & Wilson, M. J. (2024). *Turbidivision: a machine vision application for estimating turbidity from underwater images*. PeerJ, 12, e18254. https://doi.org/10.7717/peerj.18254

## Real-World Underwater Datasets

**UIEB** : Li, C., et al. (2020). *An Underwater Image Enhancement Benchmark Dataset and Beyond*. IEEE TIP, 29, 4376–4389. https://doi.org/10.1109/TIP.2019.2955241

**LSUI** : Peng, L., Zhu, C., & Bian, L. (2023). *U-Shape Transformer for Underwater Image Enhancement*. IEEE TIP, 32, 3066–3079. https://doi.org/10.1109/TIP.2023.3276332
